A common formula for calculating Euler's phi (totient) function:
(where $p$ are the distinct prime divisors of $n$)

$$
\varphi(N) = n \times \prod_{p|n} \left(1 - \frac{1}{p}\right)
$$

Example:

$$
\varphi(6) = 6 \times (1 - 1/2) \times (1 - 1/3) = 2
$$
